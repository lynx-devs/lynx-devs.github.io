ABOUT US

Who we are

lynx-devs is a startup studio based in México, building digital products with intention. Like the lynx — we're precise, adaptable, and efficient.

We believe the best products come from shipping fast and iterating relentlessly. EncuentraVet and miau.com.mx were both built to solve real problems for real people — and both were launched lean, without outside funding.

If you have an idea that needs to exist in the world, we'd love to talk, it does not matter if it is big or small, we are here to help you.
